Frequently Asked Questions

How can I test ride a Goose bike? ‚ñº

You can test ride our bikes at any authorized Goose Bikes dealer. We also host demo events throughout the year where you can try our full range of bikes on actual trails. Check our social media or contact us for upcoming demo days near you.

What warranty do Goose Bikes offer? ‚ñº

All Goose Bikes frames come with a limited lifetime warranty for the original owner. Components are covered by a 2-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Please register your bike on our website after purchase to activate your warranty.

Do you ship internationally? ‚ñº

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. For specific information about shipping to your country, please contact our customer service team.

How do I become a Goose Bikes dealer? ‚ñº

We're always looking for quality bike shops to partner with. Please select "Become a Dealer" in the contact form subject line and provide details about your shop. Our dealer relations team will get back to you with information about our dealer program.
