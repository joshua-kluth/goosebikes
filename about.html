About Goose Bikes

We're passionate mountain bikers creating the bikes we've always wanted to ride. Purpose-built, no-nonsense machines designed for serious trail enthusiasts.

Our Story

Goose Bikes was born from frustration. As avid mountain bikers, we were tired of bikes that compromised performance for mass market appeal. Every bike seemed to be a jack of all trades but master of none.

In 2020, our small team of experienced engineers and passionate riders set out to build the ultimate mountain bike - one with no compromises, cutting-edge technology, and purpose-built for serious riders.

We named our company after the wild goose - a symbol of determination, precision, and the free spirit of adventure. Like the goose, our bikes are built for performance, with every detail meticulously engineered.

Today, we remain committed to our founding principles: creating high-performance dual suspension mountain bikes that incorporate the latest technology without the marketing hype. Just pure, unadulterated trail-destroying machines.
