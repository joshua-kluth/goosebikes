Our Technology

Innovation and engineering excellence drive everything we do. Discover the technology that makes our bikes perform at the highest level.

Innovation That Matters

At Goose Bikes, we don't innovate just for the sake of it. Every technological advancement we make is driven by a single goal: to create the most capable, responsive, and reliable mountain bikes possible. Our engineering team combines decades of experience with cutting-edge research to develop technologies that deliver tangible benefits on the trail.

Adaptive Suspension Platform

Our proprietary dual-link suspension design delivers exceptional small bump sensitivity while maintaining pedaling efficiency and big-hit capability. The unique leverage curve provides a supple initial stroke that ramps up progressively, giving you confidence to push harder in technical terrain.

Each bike's kinematics are carefully tuned to match its intended use, whether that's trail riding, enduro racing, or downhill shredding.

Key Features:

  • Progressive leverage ratio for bottomless feel
  • Anti-squat tuning for efficient pedaling
  • Low-friction bearings for increased sensitivity
  • Tool-free adjustment system
  • Custom-tuned shock valving for each model

Advanced Frame Design

Our frames are engineered using the latest computational fluid dynamics and finite element analysis tools to optimize strength-to-weight ratio and ride characteristics. We strategically place material only where it's needed, creating frames that are both light and incredibly durable.

The result is a bike that responds instantaneously to rider input while maintaining stability at high speeds and in rough terrain.

Key Features:

  • Optimized tube profiles for ideal stiffness balance
  • Integrated cable routing for clean aesthetics
  • Oversized pivot bearings for long service life
  • Geometry optimized for modern trail riding
  • Lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects

Precision Drivetrain Integration

We design our bikes to work seamlessly with modern drivetrains, optimizing chain lines and suspension kinematics for smooth, quiet operation even in the most demanding conditions. Our unique chainstay design provides ample tire clearance while maintaining optimal chainring sizes.

Every bike features anti-chain-slap protection and integrated chain guides for worry-free performance in rough terrain.

Key Features:

  • Compatible with 1x drivetrains for simplicity and reliability
  • Optimized anti-squat characteristics
  • Silent operation through integrated chain damping
  • Clearance for up to 34T chainrings
  • Threaded bottom bracket for easy maintenance

Carbon Fiber

High-Performance Bearings
